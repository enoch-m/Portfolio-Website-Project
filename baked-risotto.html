Baked Asparagus Risotto

6 servings 227 kcal / serving Ready in 50 min

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups arborio rice
  • 5 cups low-sodium vegetable broth (or chicken broth), divided
  • 12 oz asparagus, tough ends trimmed, cut 1-inch
  • 2 cloves garlic, grated
  • ½ cup freshly-grated Parmesan (plus more to serve)
  • ½ + ⅛ tsp kosher salt
  • ¾ cup frozen peas, thawed
  • Freshly-ground black pepper, to finish

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 °F. Put rice in a Dutch oven.
  2. Bring broth to a simmer; blanch asparagus 4–6 min, set aside.
  3. Add 4 cups hot broth, salt & garlic to rice, cover and bake 35 min.
  4. Blend reserved 1 cup broth with peas until smooth.
  5. Stir pea purée & Parmesan into rice 2 min until creamy; fold in asparagus, adjust seasoning.

Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 227 kcal · Carbs 45.5 g · Protein 8.5 g · Fat 2.5 g · Fiber 3.5 g
